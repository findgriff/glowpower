Privacy Policy

How Glow Power collects, uses, and protects personal data.

Last updated: 25 February 2026

Who We Are

Glow Power is a trading name of Ares Sentinel Limited, a UK commercial energy brokerage. If you contact us, we act as data controller for information you provide through our website forms, email, or phone.

What We Collect

  • Contact details (name, email, phone number).
  • Business details (company name, location, spend range, requirements).
  • Technical data such as browser type and page usage for site performance and security.

How We Use Data

  • To respond to enquiries and provide procurement support.
  • To prepare and communicate quote options.
  • To maintain service records and comply with legal obligations.
  • To improve site performance and user experience.

Lawful Basis

We process data based on legitimate interests, contractual necessity where applicable, and legal obligations. Where consent is required, we request it explicitly.

Data Sharing

We may share relevant business information with energy suppliers and service providers involved in quote delivery. We do not sell personal data.

Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as required for the purposes outlined above, including legal, accounting, and compliance obligations.

Your Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of your data, and you may object to processing in certain circumstances. You can also lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office.

Contact

For privacy requests, contact info@glowpower.co.uk.
