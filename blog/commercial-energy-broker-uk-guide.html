How Commercial Energy Brokers Work in the UK

Commercial energy brokers help businesses source contracts by comparing supplier offers and advising on structure, timing, and risk. A strong broker should do more than collect prices, they should interpret options against your commercial goals.

How Brokers Are Paid

In most UK arrangements, broker commission is included in the supplier unit rate. That makes transparent disclosure critical. Before appointment, ask exactly how commission is calculated and where it appears in documentation.

What Good Broker Support Includes

  • Clear market coverage and supplier panel access.
  • Contract recommendations tied to your usage profile.
  • Plain-language explanation of fixed versus flexible options.
  • Support through contracting and supplier onboarding.

Questions to Ask Before You Appoint

  • How many suppliers are you approaching for this exercise?
  • How is your commission disclosed in my quote pack?
  • What are the trade-offs between each contract structure?
  • Who owns service after the contract is signed?

When procurement stakes are high, the broker relationship should be structured around transparency, not just headline pricing.
