About Glow Power

Independent commercial energy procurement for UK businesses that need strategic buying, not just comparison quotes.

Who We Are

Glow Power is a specialist commercial energy broker supporting high-consumption organisations across data centre, industrial, property, healthcare, and multi-site portfolios. We combine supplier access with practical contract strategy to help clients buy energy with confidence.

Our team focuses on long-term outcomes: budget certainty, market timing, and clear disclosure on every recommendation.

What Makes Us Different

Whole-of-Market Access

We source from a wide supplier panel and align options to your risk profile, load pattern, and operational priorities.

Transparent Commission

Commission is built into supplier rates and disclosed in full on every quote in line with current TPI transparency requirements.

Sector-Specific Expertise

From AI workloads to manufacturing baseload demand, we tailor procurement to each sector's actual energy behaviour.

Relationship-Led Delivery

You get dedicated account support through renewal planning, supplier onboarding, and ongoing contract lifecycle management.

Our Approach

  • Assess demand profile, contract position, and objectives.
  • Run structured market engagement across relevant suppliers.
  • Present options with clear commercial implications.
  • Support contracting, onboarding, and renewal planning.
